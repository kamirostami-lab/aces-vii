Mel Jones is a dynamic leader with a strong background in government relations, stakeholder engagement and policy development. As Director, Mel oversees the operational excellence of Ace Strategies while driving key client accounts in the health and social policy sectors.

With decades of experience on the front line of public policy development, stakeholder engagement and political strategy, Mel brings rigorous analytical depth to engagement planning and issues management.

She has successfully led major community consultation programs for infrastructure projects, ensuring social licence is earned and maintained. Mel is passionate about fostering inclusive approaches and champions these values in her client work.
