An award-winning journalist, Kevin has led newsrooms and government offices throughout Australia and is a renowned strategist for political and industry leaders. For decades, Kevin has navigated the intersection of government relations, media influence, and strategic communications.

Kevin has served as a senior media adviser to Premiers and Ministers, navigating some of the state's most significant policy challenges. His calm, evidence-based approach to issues management has made him a trusted confidant to CEOs and boards across the country.

At Ace Strategies, Kevin specialises in media training, issues management, and high-level government advocacy. His vast network in media and government ensures that clients are heard by the right people at the right time.
