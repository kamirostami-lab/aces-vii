Guy is an experienced defence and national security professional with a deep knowledge of the Indo-Pacific region and extensive Asia networks. He has worked in government and the business sector in senior advisory and executive roles.

A former senior adviser to Australia's Minister for Defence and to the Secretary of the Department of Home Affairs, Guy has built practical relationships between Australia and countries including India, Indonesia, Japan, South Korea, Timor-Leste, Vietnam and Pacific Island nations.

Based in Sydney, Guy's strategic foresight helps clients anticipate policy shifts and adapt their strategies for success in an evolving Indo-Pacific environment.
