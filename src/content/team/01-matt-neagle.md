Matt Neagle is the Founder and Managing Director of Ace Strategies. He brings extensive expertise in government relations, strategic communications, and media management to provide clients with actionable, strategic counsel.

With a career spanning journalism, political advisory roles, and corporate strategy, Matt possesses a unique ability to translate complex requirements into compelling narratives that resonate with government and community stakeholders.

Prior to establishing Ace Strategies, Matt served as a senior adviser to government ministers and held key strategic roles in major national projects. His deep understanding of the Australian political landscape enables him to provide clients with informed and impactful advice.

Matt is a founding member of the Invest Australia Alliance, a network dedicated to facilitating international investment and market entry into Australia.
