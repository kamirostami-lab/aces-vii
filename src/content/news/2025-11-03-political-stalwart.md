Founder and Managing Director Matt Neagle said Kevin Naughton is one of the most accomplished political and media strategists in Australia.

Kevin is an award-winning journalist; he has led newsrooms and government offices throughout Australia and is a renowned strategist for political and industry leaders. For decades Kevin has navigated the intersection of government relations, media influence, and strategic communications.

Mr Naughton has worked as a broadcaster, Chief of Staff and Senior Writer at the Sunday Mail, Business Editor for InDaily, and led political offices for Liberal, Labor and Independent Leaders.
